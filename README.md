<font size="7">Oxygen Concentrator</font>

The pandemic was a rough time for everyone. From losing loved ones, to being trapped indoors with uncertainty in everything we do. The healthcare system was struggling to keep pace with the rise in cases which lead to shortage of oxygen cans in India. All of us are aware of the fact that the best chance for someone to survive is by providing them with a respirator. In this crisis, I decided to use my engineering mind to innovate a solution to help our fellow human beings. Working around the clock and sourcing essential hardware I came up with a prototype for an oxygen concentrator. The device was built out of my garage to provide oxygen rich air. I was able to achieve medically approved concentration levels at the desired output flow rate. The device could not be taken to the market as it required extensive testing and paperwork, nonetheless, I discussed my idea and execution with people from various industries hoping to ignite a spark of innovation to contribute to something big, bigger than just our lives. Creating change is not easy but leading the way by example is a small step towards it.
